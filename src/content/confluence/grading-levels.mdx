# Grading & Classifying Level Quality

<ConceptCard title="Grading Levels" icon="📊">
The systematic evaluation of a trade setup based on a predefined set of criteria to determine its probability of success.
</ConceptCard>

## The Grading System

Not every Order Block or FVG is worth trading. By applying a scoring system, you can objectify your trading and avoid taking low-probability setups.

<ConfluenceMeter grade="A" score={5} maxScore={5}>
  <ul>
    <li>Aligned with HTF Trend</li>
    <li>Swept liquidity prior to forming</li>
    <li>Clear displacement (strong momentum)</li>
    <li>Unmitigated (fresh level)</li>
    <li>Aligns with a major session timing (London/NY Open)</li>
  </ul>
</ConfluenceMeter>

<ConfluenceMeter grade="C" score={2} maxScore={5}>
  <ul>
    <li>Counter-trend</li>
    <li>No clear liquidity sweep</li>
    <li>Weak displacement</li>
  </ul>
</ConfluenceMeter>
