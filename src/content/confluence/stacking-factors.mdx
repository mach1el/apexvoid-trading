# How to Stack Multiple Factors

<ConceptCard title="Stacking Factors" icon="🥞">
The process of identifying multiple independent technical reasons to take a trade at a specific price level.
</ConceptCard>

## Building a Thesis

A strong trade thesis should be able to answer "why" from multiple angles. 

<ConfluenceMeter grade="A" score={4} maxScore={5}>
  <ul>
    <li>HTF Market Structure is Bullish</li>
    <li>Price is retracing into an HTF Discount Zone</li>
    <li>An untriggered Order Block exists at the level</li>
    <li>A Liquidity Sweep just occurred below a previous session low</li>
  </ul>
</ConfluenceMeter>

<Callout type="trap" title="Common Mistake: Forced Confluence">
Don't bend the rules or "imagine" factors just to validate a trade you emotionally want to take. Let the chart speak to you.
</Callout>

<KeyTakeaways>
- Look for independent factors that support the same bias.
- Higher Timeframe context + Lower Timeframe execution = Strong Confluence.
- If the factors are weak or forced, skip the trade.
</KeyTakeaways>
