# What Confluence Is & Why It Matters

<ConceptCard title="Confluence" icon="🔗">
Confluence occurs when two or more technical factors align at the same price zone, increasing the probability of a successful trade.
</ConceptCard>

## The Power of Stacking Probabilities

No single technical concept (like an Order Block or a Fair Value Gap) works 100% of the time. The market is driven by probabilities, not certainties. Confluence is the method of stacking these probabilities in your favor.

When you find a price level that aligns with market structure, liquidity, and time (e.g., session open), you're no longer guessing; you're trading a high-probability scenario.
